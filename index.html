Full
Accreditation

TheUniversit Librale de Paris(Paris-U) is a private, accredited, independent higher education provider. Paris-U offers post-graduate programs that are different from the French National Curricula and Programmes leading to French Government accredited university qualifications, which fall under the French Public Higher Education sector. All qualifications from Paris-U are issued by Paris-U, not by the French government. All our programs are fully accredited internationally.
